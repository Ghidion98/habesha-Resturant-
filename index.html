About Us

Habesha Restaurant offers an authentic and vibrant taste of Eritrean and Ethiopian cuisine. We are welcoming and warm atmosphere, our restaurant invites you to experience the rich culinary traditional of the Horn of Africa. Our menu features an array of flavorful dishes, made from the finest ingredients, and prepared with love and passion. From savory stews like Doro Wat (spicy chicken stew) to T'ej (honey wine), every bite at Habesha Restaurant transports you to the heart of Eritrean and Ethiopian culture. We specialize in traditional dishes like Injera, Kitfo, and Shiro, ensuring every meal is a celebration of heritage and flavor. Whether you're enjoying a casual meal with friends or celebrating a special occasion, our restaurant is the perfect destination for those who appreciate bold, diverse, and mouthwatering food.

Visit our restaurant to enjoy a variety of delicious dishes, prepared with the finest ingredients and traditional recipes.
